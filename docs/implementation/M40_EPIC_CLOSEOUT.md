## 1. Closure Decision

M40 is closed as `COMPLETE_AND_FROZEN`. Its completed result is a
constitutionally reviewed vocabulary and ownership foundation for canonical
Asset and market-context measures, together with synchronized canonical
language and a reconciled Decision Log.

This is a governance closeout. It does not convert the M40 planning document
into implementation authority, approve a formula or production method, or
begin any runtime realization.

## 2. Objective and Outcome

M40's governance objective was to establish the minimum coherent language
needed to distinguish:

- source-reported Market Observations from platform-computed descriptive
  market facts;
- calculation completion from degraded state, quality evaluation, or
  judgment;
- exact observation inputs and result lineage from provider, persistence, or
  execution concerns; and
- Market Intelligence-owned business meaning from the governance rules that
  keep every concept singly owned.

That objective is complete. M40 established canonical vocabulary and
ownership boundaries only. It did not implement calculation methods,
registries, kernels, integrations, endpoints, storage, or public exposure.

2. Calculated Market Measure;
3. Computation Outcome;
4. Observation Input Manifest;
5. Market Measure Result;
6. Input Sufficiency;
7. Deterministic Calculation; and

received independent constitutional approval. The
[Decision Log](../engineering/DECISION_LOG.md#m40--constitutional-vocabulary-cycle-completion)
records the completed vocabulary cycle without duplicating its specifications.
